Your next great career move starts here. We're looking for a Part Time Learning Services Sales Associate! This is a fully Remote role, offering you the flexibility to work from anywhere. This position requires a strong and diverse skillset in relevant areas to drive success. A salary of a competitive salary is offered, reflecting your skills and experience.

 

 

About Marker Learning: 1 in 5 students has a learning disability such as dyslexia, which makes it harder for them to read, spell and write-despite their intelligence. While receiving a proper evaluation for a learning disability could be the most impactful event in a student's life, only 4% of students receive a diagnosis because of the high costs of evaluations and lack of access to qualified... psychologists. Undiagnosed students are 3x more likely to drop out of high school, 5x more likely to be unemployed, and tend to experience elevated levels of anxiety and depression. It's estimated that early diagnosis of dyslexia and other learning disabilities could save the US over 72 billion dollars of economic impact and drastically improve the lives of millions of people. Marker Learning is on a mission to make learning disability (e.g. dyslexia) assessments and diagnosis easier and more affordable for schools and parents. By leveraging proprietary technology developed by leading experts at Johns Hopkins, the end-to-end evaluation is completed in half the time and at 1/10th the cost of typical in person evaluations. We are a rapidly growing start up, backed by some of the most mission driven and reputable venture capital firms and education non-profits. Join us in our mission to inspire and support all students on their unique learning paths. About the Role At Marker, we know that a learning disability diagnosis is just the beginning of the journey. As Marker's Learning Services Sales Associate, you'll be the \"Learning Navigator\" directly consulting with Marker's clients and driving adoption of Marker's emerging suite of tutoring and coaching services. In this role, you will interface with individuals and families, taking a consultative sales approach to help our clients determine the most effective support options for their unique needs. You will be responsible for helping to execute Marker's downstream services sales strategy, providing feedback and insights to improve our sales tactics, and supporting the overall growth of our downstream offerings. Most importantly, you will embody Marker's core values, ensuring that we always put students first. If you are a go-getter with a passion for supporting neurodiverse individuals on their educational journey, we'd love to hear from you! What you'll do:  Help Marker's clients determine what type of support service (e.g. tutoring, coaching, etc.) would be most beneficial for them  Attend learning services calls with Marker evaluation clients and new customers, taking a consultative approach to selling our support services  Engage and form a genuine connection with Marker's customers, getting them excited about our offerings, recommending and selling solutions  Become an expert on Marker's support programs and product offerings  Manage to quarterly sales targets and carefully track sales outcomes, objection reasons, and conversion rates  Test and iterate on new ideas, using a \"fail-fast\" approach to support rapid innovation  Communicate and collaborate with key stakeholders across the organization Qualities of an ideal candidate:  Significant knowledge about the U.S. special education system, including IEPs, 504 plans, and various types of interventions (e.g. Orton Gillingham for dyslexia)  Experience working in sales preferred, but not required  Evening and weekend availability required, ideally after 5pm EST on weekdays and flexible availability on Saturdays and Sundays  Motivated by Marker Learning's mission to expand access to high quality and affordable support for students with learning disabilities  Highly empathetic and personable, with exceptional listening skills  Organized, detail-oriented, and efficient  Ability to learn, adapt, and thrive in a dynamic environment  Bias for action and a positive, \"roll up your sleeves\" attitude Hourly Position (1099 Contractor): $20-30 per hour base, plus sales-based commission*  Role could eventually lead to a salaried position Interested?
