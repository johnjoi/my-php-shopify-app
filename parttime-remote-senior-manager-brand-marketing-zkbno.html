Now Brewing  senior manager, Brand Marketing (Remote - U.S.)! #tobeapartner From the beginning, Starbucks set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only... celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection. The brand marketing team is responsible for building brand love and affinity for this world-class brand, inspiring & engaging Starbucks customers and partners (employees). You are a creative, data-driven marketer who has helped lead brand strategy and marketing for some of the world's leading consumer brands. You're a champion of creative excellence, strategic thinker, a master storyteller, and a teammate with a growth mindset. You're excited by ambiguity and you're everyone's favorite collaborator, listening and leaning from a diverse team of partners (employees) who span multiple disciplines across the enterprise. As a senior manager, Brand Marketing, you will...  Define the company's seasonal brand marketing goals and priorities to build brand affinity & trust and drive the business.  Lead omni-channel brand marketing campaigns from strategy through execution, measurement, and reporting.  Establish and operationalize foundational brand tools to help build brand understanding and stewardship across the company, driving integration of our brand principles into all parts of the customer and partner experience.  Gain a deep understanding of customer and partner audiences, trends and brand health. Work closely with our insights and media teams to report on brand health and quantify brand marketing's impact on the business.  Partner with key stakeholders across marketing, creative studio, internal and external communications, DAIBO (analytics and insights), operations, and international marketing to operationalize brand strategy and accelerate the growth of this iconic brand.  Identify, evaluate, and action new opportunities to increase brand affinity.
