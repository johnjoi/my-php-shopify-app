Our award-winning solution allows users to discover and purchase products on the publisher sites they visit daily. For publishers, we power white-labeled e-commerce shops with on-site... checkout for readers. The Role: We are looking for an experienced Senior Sales Account Executive (AE) to join our team. As an AE at StackCommerce, you will have the opportunity to work with some of the biggest media brands in the industry while also being part of a fast-paced and innovative company culture. We offer fantastic benefits such as health, dental, flexible vacation time and remote working. As an AE, some of your job responsibilities may include (but are not limited to): What You'll Do:  Building and managing relationships with Brands and Media Companies  Identifying and close new business opportunities (SDR support and Self-Sourced)  Meeting and exceeding sales targets  Developing and executing strategic sales plans  Collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive revenue growth  Representing the StackCommerce brand in the media industry  Providing exceptional customer service to clients  Continuously staying up-to-date on industry trends and developments  Leveraging data and analytics to inform sales strategy  Utilizing the StackAgency platform to create custom solutions for clients As a member of the StackCommerce team, you will have the opportunity to grow your career and make a real impact on the company's success. We are passionate about our work and are committed to creating a positive and collaborative work environment where everyone can succeed. What You'll Bring:  Bachelor's Degree or above in a relevant field (Business, Marketing, Finance, Analytics a plus).  Min 3-5 years Sales experience in related fields - Digital Marketing, Media Sales, and/or Affiliate Marketing (Sales)  Deep knowledge of the Affiliate Marketing landscape. Strong understanding of CPC, CPA, CPL, and Flat Fee sales  Strong book of business in affiliate marketing a plus, but not a requirement  Personality Traits:  Strong organizational skills  Excellent communication skills  Competitive natured and driven by goals/targets  Thrives in a team environment. Comfortable jumping in and assisting the Account Management team to execute campaigns and drive renewals  Self-starter & comfortable in a fast paced environment  Skills:  Deep understanding of Digital Marketing, Digital Media, Content Commerce, and Affiliate Marketing  Expert in outbound sales efforts including but not limited to outbound email campaigns, drip campaigns, warm and cold calling and able to provide direction and support to SDR team members  Comfortable presenting to Sr. level executives and decision makers at both top brands and publishers  Strong interpersonal skills  Basic understanding of sales forecasting and sales projections based on pipeline What to Expect:  Intro Call: Youll hop on a quick call with the hiring manager so we can get to know you better  and you can learn a little more about the role.  Remote-Team Interview: Youll be meeting with 2-3 team members on a video call.
