Job Summary A System Support technician is responsible for all aspects of support and technical assistance by addressing a wide variety of user issues including software problems, hardware failures and network issues. Duties and Responsibilities  Respond to end users quickly and accurately while delivering quality customer service by providing a single point of contact for reporting technical incidents or making inquiries.  Identify end user incidents then apply the proper troubleshooting so that the incident can be resolved on initial contact or escalate non-resolvable incidents accordingly.  Provide technical support in the resolution of customer incidents and inquires, in a pleasant, professional, and timely manner, which is beneficial to customers.  Record all activities relating to customer calls and attempts to resolve incidents or satisfy requests using available support tools and technologies, and for tracking incidents through resolution.  Coordinate repair efforts with hardware vendors when applicable.  Verify end users through proper channels to eliminate risk to the corporation.  Navigate several applications simultaneously.  Connect remotely to further gather information and resolve issues.  Regularly follow up on aging tickets assigned.  Flexibility in work schedule required to provide coverage, when needed. Qualifications  Associate degree in a technical discipline or 2-4 years of technical equivalent work experience  Strong Customer Service skills  Demonstrate a practical and data-driven approach to problem-solving  Self-motivated demonstrating superior technical ability  Working knowledge of MS Office and Office 365, Active Directory, Remote Connection software such as Bomgar and Cisco VPN  Ability to prioritize and manage multiple responsibilities that are time-sensitive With us, youll enjoy:  Competitive wages and annual bonus opportunity  Medical, dental, vision and life insurance  401(k) with a company match  Paid vacation and holidays  Tuition reimbursement  A wide variety of discounts on technology, travel, food and fuel  Opportunity for growth and advancement with company paid training Not all benefit plans are available to all team members.
