Microsoft is uniquely positioned to win Developer workloads to help with our customers digital transformation journey. The Microsoft Developer portfolio spans across Azure Developer Services, GitHub/Azure DevOps and Visual Studio.

As the Solution Sales Specialist for Azure Developer Audience, you will act as a technical sales professional within our enterprise sales organization with a special focus on Development workloads (Azure Developer Services, GitHub/Azure DevOps, Visual Studio). You are seen as a key member of the virtual team responsible for building a compelling strategy for growing the Developer share within key accounts in both revenue and Azure consumption. You will engage in executive conversation both in and outside of technical teams to provide customers with insights and solutions leveraging the Microsoft Developer Platform and Developer Services offerings that drive business transformation through key technologies.

Microsofts mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. As employees we come together with a growth mindset, innovate to empower others and collaborate to realize our shared goals. Each day we build on our values of respect, integrity, and accountability to create a culture of inclusion where everyone can thrive at work and beyond.

Responsibilities

  • You will be the key sales leader and influencer in shaping customer decisions to buy, build and transform new markets and lead transformational shifts in our customers.
  • You will lead and challenge customers with clear strategic vision of Modern Engineering practices using the Microsoft and GitHub Platforms.
  • You will orchestrate and lead Microsoft's field team to win the Microsoft and GitHub development platform solution sales opportunities.
  • You will develop, communicate, and provide high business impact solutions that enable digital transformation.
  • You will be a leader of the sales opportunity team, disciplined in business-management and build an active business network to meet and exceed sales targets and operational standards.
  • You will influence the Microsoft and GitHub go-to-market strategies by providing feedback to sales, marketing, and engineering on current and future product requirements and sales blockers you encounter.

Qualifications

Required/Minimum Qualifications

  • 3+ years technology-related sales or account management experience
  • OR Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology, or related field AND 2+ years technology-related sales or account management experience.
  • 3+ years experience in one or more of the following:
    • Application development platforms on public clouds and/or Azure in development languages (i.e. Java, JavaScript, Python, PHP, C#, Node.JS targeting Linux, Windows, public clouds, or Azure).
    • DevOps practices, including continuous planning/Agile, CI/CD, quality, and security with DevOps toolchains (i.e., Azure DevOps, GitHub, GitLab, Atlassian).
    • Integrated development environments (e.g. Visual Studio, Visual Studio Code, Eclipse, XCode, WebSphere) targeting Linux, Windows, public clouds (i.e. Azure, AWS, Google).
