What were looking for Toyotas Enterprise Compliance Department is looking for a passionate and highly motivated Privacy Compliance Analyst. The primary responsibility of this role is to provide support for compliance with GLBAs Financial Privacy Rule and ensure the organization maintains compliant data-sharing practices. Reporting to the Privacy National Manager, the person in this role will support the Enterprise Compliance department's objective to advance the Privacy Compliance Program and enhance the organization's overall Compliance Management System. What youll be doing Facilitate GLBA privacy noticing for the organization by maintaining up-to-date notices; documenting business rules for delivery of notices; partnering with data teams to implement logic for GLBA preference management; and managing relationship with print and fulfillment vendor. Conducting regular audits and assessments to ensure compliance with GLBA noticing requirements. Document and maintain records of privacy notices and compliance activities; prepare regular compliance reports. Identify and address gaps or risks related to privacy notices and information-sharing practices. Provide specialized support for the organizations data-sharing and data acquisition practices by conducting initial reviews of data requests and facilitating necessary additional reviews by relevant stakeholders; maintaining related trackers; generating reporting; facilitating weekly meetings; and conducting regular audits and assessments.
