About the Team DoorDash Labs is an independent team within DoorDash. We explore robotics and automation to transform last mile logistics in the long term. If you have a passion for applying robotics solutions in a service used by millions of people, then we want to talk to you... About the Role We're looking for a technical operator to carry out the testing, public road deployment, and logging of new advanced technologies, working in various offices around the Bay Area. In this role, you'll report into the Team Lead and be a core member of our Remote Operations Team. You're excited about this opportunity because you will...  Conduct remote operation and assessment of autonomous robots on real-world durability test routes or tracks, while carrying out specialized testing procedures.  Collaborate closely with various stakeholders, such as hardware, product, software, and business operations teams, to enhance the product.  Perform tests, record outcomes, assess issues comprehensively, classify their severity, replicate scenarios through controlled and field tests, and deliver feedback to the engineering team.  Meticulous focus on process adherence, testing protocols, and checklists to guarantee elevated levels of safety, efficiency, and reliability for new products.  Utilize internal tools to analyze logs, trace issues, document findings, and diligently track them until resolution is achieved.  Conduct analysis and generate daily reports that comprehensively outline observations and identified issues. We're excited about you because...  2 years of experience or strong interest in live Autonomy Operations for public road deployment  Must be willing to work flexible hours including weekends and operating micro-mobility  Must demonstrate the ability to communicate clearly both written and orally  Familiarity with issue reporting/ticketing tools to collect feedback, schedule fixes, and track progress  Experience with debugging and triaging complex systems such as autonomous robots About DoorDash At DoorDash, our mission to empower local economies shapes how our team members move quickly, learn, and reiterate in order to make impactful decisions that display empathy for our range of users-from Dashers to merchant partners to consumers. We are a technology and logistics company that started with door-to-door delivery, and we are looking for team members who can help us go from a company that is known for delivering food to a company that people turn to for any and all goods. DoorDash is growing rapidly and changing constantly, which gives our team members the opportunity to share their unique perspectives, solve new challenges, and own their careers.
