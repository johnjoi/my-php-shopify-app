The team owns the data assets for all Guest facing surfaces and builds frameworks to scale data informed decision making. In this role, you will work with some of Airbnbs most impactful data assets, and have a direct hand in enabling measurement of Airbnb product launches. The Difference You Will Make As a Senior Analytics Engineer, you will bring a unique lens to our data strategy and provide in-depth technical expertise to the team. We are looking for someone with hands-on experience in data modeling, metric development, and large-scale distributed data processing frameworks like Trino and Spark. Using these tools, along with first-class internal data tooling, you will scale product and AI/ML teams capabilities to unlock insights and develop high quality models.
