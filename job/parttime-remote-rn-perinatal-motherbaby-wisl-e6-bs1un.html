Department: ICE | WISL Nursing Srv Perinatal IP C Department's Website: Summary of Job Duties: This posting will be used to fill multiple positions Full Time: Shifts Vary The Registered Nurse provides direct patient care to assigned patients in our Perinatal Unit (E6). The UAMS Perinatal unit is in the Harry P. Ward Clinical Tower. The unit consists of 28 rooms, and a newborn procedure area. E6 Perinatal provides high-risk obstetrical care for patients all over the state, including comprehensive care for postpartum, well newborn, high risk Antepartum, and Gynecologic patients. E6 Perinatal supports patient and family centered care and as a result, the newborns, if stable, are allowed to stay with their mothers after delivery to promote bonding. Qualifications: Minimum: Graduate of an accredited Nursing Program with Associates Degree, Nursing Diploma, Bachelors in Nursing, or Master's in Nursing. Valid RN License. One (1) year Registered Nursing experience. Excellent communication skills. Basic computer skills. Strong work ethics and accountability.
