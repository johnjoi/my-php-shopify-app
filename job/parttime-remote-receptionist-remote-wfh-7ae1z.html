**Job Title: Remote Receptionist** We're on the lookout for a dedicated and enthusiastic Receptionist to join our remote team! If you're a pro at juggling tasks in a high-volume environment and have a knack for Salesforce, we'd love to hear from you... What You'll Do  **Welcome & Greet:** Be the first point of contact, greeting clients and visitors with a warm, professional, and friendly demeanor - even virtually!  **Communication Hub:** Answer and direct phone calls promptly and efficiently, ensuring everyone feels valued and heard.  **Schedule Management:** Manage and schedule appointments like a pro, keeping everything running smoothly.  **Reception Area Care:** Maintain a clean and welcoming virtual reception area, reflecting our commitment to excellence.  **Admin Support:** Assist with administrative tasks as needed, showing your versatility and can-do attitude.  **Salesforce Savvy:** Utilize Salesforce to manage client information and track interactions, keeping everything organized and up-to-date. What We're Looking For  **Experience:** Previous experience as a receptionist, preferably in a high-volume setting, to ensure you're comfortable in a fast-paced environment.  **Tech Skills:** Proficiency in Salesforce is highly preferred, as you'll be using it daily to keep our processes streamlined.  **Communication Pro:** Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to build relationships and represent us with professionalism.  **Organizational Guru:** Strong organizational and multitasking abilities to keep multiple plates spinning without breaking a sweat.  **Professionalism:** A professional appearance and attitude to reflect our brand positively.  **Discretion:** Ability to handle sensitive information with discretion, maintaining confidentiality at all times. What You'll Get  **Future Planning:** 401(k) to help you save for the future.  **Health & Wellness:** Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance to keep you feeling your best.  **Support:** Employee assistance program to lend a helping hand when you need it.  **Time Off:** Paid time off to relax and recharge.
