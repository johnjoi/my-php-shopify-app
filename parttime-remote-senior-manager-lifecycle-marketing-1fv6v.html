Day to Day This role is part of the Growth Marketing team, under Lifecycle. The Lifecycle Team helps employers simplify hiring, connect with quality candidates, drive loyalty, and delights employers throughout their hiring journey. The Senior Manager of Lifecycle Marketing will be responsible for bringing new products and features to existing customers, and identifying growth opportunities. They are proven at team collaboration, driving feature adoption, and growth via owned channels The agile Senior Manager of Lifecycle Marketing adjusts strategies based on customer needs, partnering and leveraging best practices. Skills/Competencies  6+ years experience in building strategy for cross-channel marketing campaigns including (but not limited to) email, retargeting, SMS, and in-product notifications.
